About Momentum

Momentum is your ultimate habit tracker, designed to help you achieve your goals and explore your potential.

Our mission is to make habit building fun, engaging, and rewarding.

Why Choose Momentum?

Momentum combines habit tracking with gamification, turning your daily routines into an interstellar adventure. With features like Habit Analytics, Mission Profiles, and Acheivements, we make productivity exciting and rewarding.

Our Project

This project was developed as part of an undergraduate software engineering class to demonstrate skills in web development. Momentum is a fully functional habit tracker that integrates gamification to engage users. This application was built with Django 5, SQLite, and Bootstrap. Deployed with Amazon EC2.
